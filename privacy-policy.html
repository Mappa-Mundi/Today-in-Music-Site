Today in Music - Privacy Policy

At Today in Music, your privacy is of paramount importance to us.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines how Today in Music ("we," "us," "our") handles your Personal Information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and only collect the minimal data necessary to enhance your experience. Your use of our application constitutes your agreement to this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

The only personal information we collect is your email address when you sign up for our services. No other personal data is collected or used.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your email address is used to keep you informed about our product and updates. We may also use it to reach out to you directly, but rest assured, your email is not sold to third parties.

4. Unsubscribing

You may unsubscribe from our communications at any time. Every email we send will include a link to opt out of future communications.

5. Retention of Information

We will retain your email address for as long as necessary to provide you with the services and communications you have requested.

6. Security of Your Information

We take the security of your information seriously and implement various security measures to protect your email address from unauthorized access or disclosure.

7. Consent

By providing us with your email address, you consent to our collection and use of it as described in this Privacy Policy.

8. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to make changes to this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
